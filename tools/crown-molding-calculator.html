Quick reference

Common spring angle settings

Standard compound miter saw settings for 90° corners. These are the two most common molding profiles you'll find at any home center.

Spring angle Corner Miter Bevel
38°Inside31.6°33.9°
38°Outside31.6°33.9°
45°Inside35.3°30.0°
45°Outside35.3°30.0°

The miter and bevel angles are the same for both inside and outside corners — what changes is the direction you rotate and tilt the saw. For inside corners, tilt the blade toward the fence. For outside corners, tilt away. Always do a test cut on scrap first.
